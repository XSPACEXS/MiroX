# Festival Campus

## Overview

- **Purpose**: A multi-day festival campus map designed as an immersive outdoor world with stages, tents, food villages, wellness zones, and activity areas spread across a park-like landscape. Each area is a themed zone with its own programming, vibe, and schedule. The campus layout creates a festival experience that attendees can explore spatially, with pathways showing walking routes, estimated travel times between stages, and real-time crowd density indicators. Think Coachella meets a tech festival.
- **Best For**: Multi-day corporate festivals and offsites, tech conference organizers with outdoor/indoor hybrid venues, music and culture festival planners, company retreat coordinators designing campus-style experiences, community event organizers.
- **Complexity**: Advanced
- **Board Size**: 5000 x 3500px

## Variations

1. **Corporate Retreat Campus**: Replace public festival with internal company offsite, add team-building activities
2. **Hackathon Campus**: Expand Tech Tent and Innovation Lab, reduce entertainment, add 24/7 hacking spaces
3. **Community Festival**: Focus on local businesses, family activities, and cultural performances
4. **Winter Festival Campus**: Indoor venues connected by heated walkways, holiday theming

## Build Instructions

1. Create board at 5000x3500px with Park Green background
2. Place festival title banner at top
3. Position Main Stage and Tech Tent in the upper area as anchor venues
4. Spread Workshop Village, Food Court, and Wellness Garden in the middle
5. Place Innovation Lab and Chill Lounge in the lower area
6. Draw sandy pathway connectors between all zones with walk times
7. Add Daily Schedule and Festival Info at the bottom
8. Mark first aid stations with red cross symbols at 3 locations

## Tips & Best Practices

- Print large-format versions of this map for physical wayfinding stations
- Use Miro's interactive features to let attendees click zones for details
- Update crowd density indicators in real-time during the event (green/yellow/red)
- Add a "Festival App" QR code on the map linking to the mobile schedule
- Photograph each zone and overlay images for post-event documentation
- Create a "Festival Passport" where attendees collect stamps from each zone
