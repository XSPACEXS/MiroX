## Isometric 3D Building Construction

### Step-by-Step Isometric Building in Miro

Since Miro does not have native isometric tools, here is how to simulate the effect:

**Simple Method (Recommended):**

1. Create a rectangle (400 x 250px) — this is the FRONT face
2. Fill with zone color, add building label text
3. Create a smaller rectangle (400 x 40px) at the top — this is the ROOF
4. Fill with zone color at 70% opacity (lighter)
5. Offset the roof 15px up and 15px right to create the isometric angle
6. Create a narrow rectangle (40 x 250px) on the right side — this is the SIDE face
7. Fill with zone color at 130% darkness (darker)
8. Position the side face flush with the right edge of the front face
9. Group all three shapes

**Advanced Method (For Hero Buildings):**

1. Follow the simple method above
2. Add a shadow: Dark gray rectangle (420 x 20px) at 15% opacity, placed 10px below the building base
3. Add entrance: Small darker rectangle (40 x 30px) at the bottom center of the front face
4. Add windows: 2-3 small white rectangles (20 x 15px) on the front face at 30% opacity
5. Add a flag: Small colored triangle on a stick on the roof

### Building Size Guide

| Building Importance          | Front Face Size | When to Use                   |
| ---------------------------- | --------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Anchor (Main Stage)          | 600 x 350px     | 1-2 per board, hero buildings |
| Major (Expo, Breakout)       | 450 x 280px     | 3-4 per board, key zones      |
| Standard (Workshop, Lounge)  | 350 x 220px     | 4-6 per board, regular zones  |
| Minor (Info Desk, First Aid) | 250 x 160px     | 2-4 per board, utility zones  |

## Pathway Design System

### The Golden Pathway (Main Route)

The signature design element of immersive event guides.

- **Width**: 5px
- **Color**: Warm Gold (#F1C40F)
- **Style**: Solid line with rounded caps
- **End markers**: Rounded arrow tips
- **Labels**: 14pt text, charcoal on gold background badge, at pathway midpoints
- **Example label**: "Main pathway — 3 min walk"

### Pathway Decoration

Along main pathways, place at regular intervals:

- Trees every 200px (alternating sides)
- Human figures every 400px (walking along the path)
- Direction markers at intersections
- Distance/time labels at pathway midpoints

### Pathway Types Comparison

| Type            | Width | Color              | Style  | Label Format     |
| --------------- | ----- | ------------------ | ------ | ---------------- |
| Main route      | 5px   | Gold #F1C40F       | Solid  | "Main pathway"   |

| Service route   | 2px   | Dark gray #636E72  | Dashed | "Staff only"     |

## Board Background Treatment

### The Showcase Sky

For the top 30-40% of the board:

- Base color matches board background
- 3-4 cloud clusters placed at varying heights
- Stars scattered (if dark background)
- Title banner floats in the sky area
- Atmosphere gradually transitions to ground level

### The Ground Plane

For the bottom 60-70% of the board:

- Subtle green tint (#F1F8E9 at 30%) over the base background suggests grass or terrain
- Pathways are rendered ON this ground plane
- Buildings sit ON the ground with shadows
- Trees and vegetation grow FROM the ground

## Mobile-First Design Considerations

Since 80%+ of event attendees access the board on mobile devices during the actual event:

### Zone Tap Targets

- Minimum zone size: 300 x 200px (so it is tappable on a phone screen)
- Zone labels: Minimum 20pt font (readable when zoomed to fit one zone on a phone screen)
- Session cards within zones: Minimum 14pt font for session titles

### Navigation Aids for Mobile

- Zone title visible at 1-zone zoom level
- "Back to overview" instruction in each zone
- Companion panel readable at full-width mobile zoom
- Color coding visible and distinguishable on small screens

## Quality Checklist

Before considering an Event Guide Immersive Isometric board visually complete:

- [ ] The board creates a "wow" reaction at first glance (full zoom-out)
- [ ] Isometric perspective is consistent across all buildings
- [ ] Golden pathways connect ALL major zones
- [ ] Every zone has a unique, identifiable color
- [ ] Decorative elements enhance atmosphere without obscuring information
- [ ] Clouds are present in the sky area (3+ clouds minimum)
- [ ] Trees line the pathways (minimum 1 tree per 300px of pathway)
- [ ] Human figures populate the world (minimum 8-10 figures)
- [ ] Session information is complete (time, title, speaker, room)
- [ ] The schedule is accessible (either within the world or linked)
- [ ] Companion zone is positioned for real-time updates
- [ ] Emergency info (first aid, exits) is clearly marked
- [ ] WiFi and practical info is visible
- [ ] The board works on mobile (large enough zones, readable text)
- [ ] Version and date are displayed
