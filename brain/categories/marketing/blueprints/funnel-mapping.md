# Marketing & Sales Funnel Mapping Board

## Overview

- **Purpose**: Visualize the complete customer journey from first touch to long-term retention, mapping tactics, content, conversion metrics, and optimization opportunities at each stage. Enables marketing and sales teams to identify leaks, align on handoff points, and prioritize investments by funnel stage.
- **Best For**: Growth marketers, demand generation leads, sales operations managers, and revenue teams seeking to optimize the full customer lifecycle.
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Board Size**: 5000x2800px

## Connectors & Flow

- Large directional arrows between each funnel stage frame (solid, #1A237E, 3px, arrowhead): Awareness -> Interest -> Consideration -> Decision -> Retention
- Each arrow is labeled with the conversion rate between stages
- Dashed arrows from each stage down to the Conversion Metrics Dashboard (dashed, #9E9E9E, 1px)
- Red dashed arrow from the three biggest leak points to the Leak Analysis frame (dashed, #FF5252, 2px)

## Example Content

The entire board is pre-filled with realistic SaaS funnel data for "AutoPilot 3.0." Each stage contains specific tactics with budget allocations, content assets with real titles, and measurable stage metrics based on realistic B2B SaaS benchmarks. The Conversion Metrics Dashboard provides actual conversion rates, targets, gaps, and revenue impact calculations. The Leak Analysis frame identifies the three most significant drop-off points with testable hypotheses.

## Variations

1. **B2C E-Commerce Funnel**: Replace trial-based Decision stage with cart/checkout optimization. Add "Browse -> Add to Cart -> Checkout -> Purchase" micro-funnel. Replace enterprise tactics with influencer marketing and UGC.
2. **PLG (Product-Led Growth) Focus**: Emphasize in-product metrics: activation rate, feature adoption, expansion triggers. Replace outbound sales with product-qualified lead (PQL) signals.
3. **Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Funnel**: Replace broad awareness tactics with targeted account lists. Add an "Account Engagement Score" frame. Replace volume metrics with account penetration rates.

## Build Instructions

1. Create board at 5000x2800px with background #F5F5F5.

3. Apply unique background colors to each stage frame as specified.
4. Add the funnel band shape at the top of each frame, colored to match the stage.
5. Populate each frame with tactics (colored sticky notes), content (yellow sticky notes), and metrics (text blocks).
6. Place the Conversion Metrics Dashboard at (50, 1500), size 4800x500.
7. Place the Leak Analysis frame at (3850, 2050), size 1000x800.
8. Draw large directional arrows between funnel stages with conversion rate labels.
9. Draw dashed arrows from stages to the dashboard.
10. Draw red dashed arrows from leak points to the Leak Analysis frame.
11. Group elements within each frame.

## Tips & Best Practices

- Update conversion rates monthly and track trends over time — a single snapshot is less valuable than a trendline.
- The Leak Analysis frame should always have exactly 3 items: focus beats breadth when optimizing funnels.
- Use this board in joint marketing-sales meetings to align on handoff criteria (when does a lead move from marketing to sales?).
- Color-code the conversion rate labels: green if above target, yellow if within 1% of target, red if below target by more than 1%.
- Pair this board with the Campaign Planning board to see how specific campaigns feed specific funnel stages.
